Our Aim and Purpose

We provide learners, especially those from disadvantaged communities, who display potential in Mathematics, Science and Information Technology, with high quality education for successful tertiary education.

What we offer

We pride ourselves on offering quality education and extra curriculars

Aviation

A quality 3 year Aviation programme run by Cape Town Flying Club. This Private Pilot License is an accredited course at the CAA of SA.

Robotics

A Robotics programme offered as part of the curriculum.

Drone Club

Together with Cape FPV Flyers, we offer drone designing and building.

Community Outreach

Community Outreach programmes via the Rotary Club and Lions International

The Confucius Classroom

Mandarin is offered as a subject and provides learners with the opportunity to further their studies in China.

Experienced and Specialised Educators

Most educators have experience as markers or chief markers in NSC examinations

Hostel Accomodation

Hostel accommodation available for 300 learners

Programming

CAMST has become a member institution of the Oracle Academy which prepares learners for international certification.
